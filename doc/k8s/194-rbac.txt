RBAC

Subjekt
  User
  Group
  Service Account
  
Role
  apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
  kind: Role
  metadata:
    name: demorole
    namespace: ns1
  rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["pods"]
    verbs: [*]

ClusterRole
  apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
  kind: ClusterRole
  metadata:
    name: democlusterrole
  rules:
  - apiGroups: [""]
    resources: ["nodes"]
    verbs: [*]

RoleBinding
  apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
  kind: RoleBinding
  metadata:
    name: demorolebinding
    namespace: ns1
  roleRef:
    apiGroup: rbac.authorization.k8s.io
    kind: Role
    name: demorole
  subjects:
  - apiGroup: rbac.authorization.k8s.io
    kind: User | Group | ServiceAccount
    name: demouser | demogroup | demosa
    namespace: ns1
  
ClusterRoleBinding
  apiVersion: rbac.authorization.k8s.io/v1
  kind: ClusterRoleBinding
  metadata:
    name: democlusterrolebinding
  roleRef:
    apiGroup: rbac.authorization.k8s.io
    kind: ClusterRole
    name: democlusterrole
  subjects:
  - apiGroup: rbac.authorization.k8s.io
    kind: User | Group | ServiceAccount
    name: demouser | demogroup | demosa

Verb
  *, get, list, create, update, patch, watch, delete, deletecollection

Default ClusteRoles
  admin, edit, view, cluster-admin, ...
 
Kombinasjoner
  Subjekt → RoleBinding → Role (pods)
  Subjekt → RoleBinding → ClusterRole (pods)
  Subjekt → ClusterRoleBinding → ClusterRole (pv, noder)
