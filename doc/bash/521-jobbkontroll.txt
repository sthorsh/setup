JOBBKONTROLL

Builtins
  jobs
  fg [jobspec] or %N
  bg [jobspec ...] or %N ... &
  kill -l or kill [-sigspec] jobspec|pid
  disown [jobspec ... | pid ...]
  suspend
  wait [jobspec ... | pid ...]
  
Spesialtegn
  ctrl-c intr: sigint
  ctrl-\ quit: sigquit
  ctrl-z susp: sigtstp
  ...

sigspec
   1 [sig]hup
   2 [sig]int
   3 [sig]quit 
   6 [sig]abrt 
   9 [sig]kill
  15 [sig]term (kills' default)
  18 [sig]cont
  20 [sig]tstp
  ...
  
jobspec
  %N      job N
  %       current job
  %%      current job
  %+      current job
  %-      previous job
  %name   command name (prefix)
  %?str   command line substring
