IMPLISITT cf https://openid.net/specs/openid-connect-core-1_0.html#ImplicitFlowAuth

1.1 EU velger login i RP

2.1 RP > OP /.well-known/openid-configuration

3.1 RP vha UA redirect sender rq til OP /auth
    > response_type=idtoken token
    > client_id=ID
    > redirect_uri=URI
    > scope=openid profile email offline_access

3.2 OP validerer rq params
    OP autentiserer EU
    OP autoriserer RP

3.3 OP vha UA redirect sender rs til RP /callback
    < access_token: AT
    < token_type: Bearer
    < expires_in: 3600
    < id_token: IT

3.4 RP validerer AT cf at_hash
    RP validerer IT

4.1 RP sender rq til OP /userinfo
    > AT

4.2 OP validerer rq params

4.3 OP returnerer rs til RP
    < Standard|opt claims

4.4 RP validerer rs params

5.1 RP sender rq til RS /api
    > AT
    > IT

5.2 RS evt vha OP validerer AT

5.3 OP returnerer rs til RP
